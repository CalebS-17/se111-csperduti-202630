All about the show Spice and Wolf

A slow-burn fantasy romance show following a traveling merchant named Kraft Lawrence and the wolf deity Holo as they journey through a medieval world. After they meet, they make a deal: Holo promises to use her wisdom to help him profit in return for safe tavel back to her homeland. The series focuses on economics, trade, and their relationship as they bond over the course of their journey.

Director: Hijiri Sanpei

Animation Studio: Passione

First Aired: April 2, 2024

Genres: Fantasy, Romance

Rating: 7.4
