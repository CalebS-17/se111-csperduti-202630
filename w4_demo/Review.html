My Review of Spice and Wolf

Caleb Sperduti April 14th, 2026

Spice and Wolf is calm, character driven show with a very cozy medieval setting. It has a fleshed out world and economic system. As someone who enjoys slow burn shows I greatly enjoyed it and found the characters to be memorable. Although I can see the show feeling boring to those who enjoy more action centered shows I found the character banter and traveling theme to be very engaging.

Check out Spice and Wolf: Merchant Meets The Wise Wolf on IMDB.com!

Trivia and fun facts for Spice and Wolf:

  • Holo is most likely based on European pagan harvest deities.

  • The series is a reboot of the original show that aired in 2008-2009.

  • The economics system featured prominently in the show was partially based on real medieval European trade and currency to help it feel realistic.
